Ukraine Crisis

Millions of people have been displaced and thousands of civilians have died over the six months of fighting in Ukraine. Due to the disruption of commercial supply lines, many people who are still living near to the frontlines are having difficulty getting access to food. People who have evacuated their houses have frequently lost their jobs and are attempting to restore their lives after running out of money or discovering that their homes have been completely destroyed.

Pakistan Floods

Due to the disastrous flooding and excessive rains during late August and beginning of September, Pakistan is currently experiencing a FOOD crisis of historic proportions. Less than 40% of Pakistan's land area is arable, but the latest flood event was so massive that around one-third of the country's land mass was inundated. As many as 33 million people, or 13% of the country, have already been uprooted and are struggling to live, while more than 3 million acres of land of crops have been destroyed or damaged.
